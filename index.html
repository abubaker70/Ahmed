About Me

I'm Ahmed babikr, a Sudanese filmmaker born and raised in Qatar. I graduated from Near East University with a Bachelor's degree in filmmaking and broadcasting. I learned the practical and theory aspects of film studies as they work together simultaneously. During my studies I developed extensive skills in cinematography, editing, directing, script writing and semiotics language.
